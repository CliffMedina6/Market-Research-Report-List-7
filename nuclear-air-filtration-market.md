<p><p>The nuclear air filtration market is divided into two main types: stationary and portable systems. Stationary air filtration units are designed for fixed installations in facilities like nuclear power plants or research labs, ensuring continuous air quality control and containment of airborne contaminants. Portable air filtration systems, on the other hand, are mobile units that can be easily relocated to different areas as needed, providing flexible solutions for emergency response or temporary setups during decontamination processes. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining safety and compliance in nuclear environments.</p></p>
