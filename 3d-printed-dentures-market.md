<p><p>The 3D printed dentures market comprises two main types: full dentures and partial dentures. Full dentures are designed for patients who have lost all their natural teeth, providing a complete set of artificial teeth for functional and aesthetic purposes. In contrast, partial dentures are used for individuals who still have some natural teeth, offering support and filling gaps to enhance chewing and speech. The adoption of 3D printing technology in both categories improves customization, fit, and production efficiency, benefiting patients and dental professionals alike.</p></p>
