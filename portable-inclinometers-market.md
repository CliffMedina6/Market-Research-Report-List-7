<p><p>The portable inclinometers market comprises three main types: mechanical, digital, and electronic inclinometers. Mechanical inclinometers utilize analog mechanisms, often featuring a gravity-driven pendulum, to measure angles of inclination. Digital inclinometers convert the angle data into a digital format, enhancing readability and accuracy. Electronic inclinometers leverage sensors and microprocessors for real-time data acquisition and analysis, offering advanced features such as data logging and connectivity. Each type addresses specific needs in various applications like construction, civil engineering, and geotechnical monitoring.</p></p>
