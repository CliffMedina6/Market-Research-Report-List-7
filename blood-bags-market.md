<p><strong>The Blood Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single</li><li>Double</li><li>Triple</li><li>Quadruple</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blood bags are categorized based on the number of compartments they contain for collecting and storing different blood components. </p><p>- **Single bags** hold whole blood. </p><p>- **Double bags** allow separation into two components, typically packed red blood cells and plasma. </p><p>- **Triple bags** enable separation into three components, such as red cells, plasma, and platelets. </p><p>- **Quadruple bags** expand this further to include an additional component, often platelets or cryoprecipitate. </p><p>Other variations may include specialized bags for specific cellular components or storage requirements.</p></p>
