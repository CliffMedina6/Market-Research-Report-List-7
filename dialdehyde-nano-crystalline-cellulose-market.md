<p><p>The Dialdehyde Nano-Crystalline Cellulose (DANC) market is primarily categorized into two types based on production methods: Sulfuric Acid Hydrolysis and Enzymatic Hydrolysis. The Sulfuric Acid Hydrolysis method involves treating cellulose with sulfuric acid to produce nano-crystalline structures, which enhances material properties like strength and stability. In contrast, the Enzymatic Hydrolysis method uses specific enzymes for a more environmentally friendly approach, yielding high-purity cellulose with minimal chemical waste. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including packaging, biomedical, and composites.</p></p>
