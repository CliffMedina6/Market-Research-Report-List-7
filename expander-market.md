<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Expander</li><li>Medium Pressure Expander</li><li>High Pressure Expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The expander market is categorized into low, medium, and high-pressure expanders based on the pressure levels at which they operate. Low-pressure expanders are typically used in applications where the inlet pressure is below 1 bar, ideal for energy recovery in low-temperature processes. Medium-pressure expanders operate in the range of 1 to 10 bars, suitable for moderate energy recovery and optimization in industrial processes. High-pressure expanders, exceeding 10 bars, are designed for high-efficiency energy conversion in power generation and heavy industrial applications.</p></p>
