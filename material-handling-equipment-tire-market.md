<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The material handling equipment tire market encompasses three primary types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering cushioning and traction, making them suitable for outdoor and rough terrain applications. Solid tires, made from rubber or resilient materials, provide durability and are ideal for indoor use due to their resistance to punctures and wear. Polyurethane tires combine the advantages of both types, offering a lightweight, long-lasting solution with low rolling resistance, suitable for various material handling tasks.</p></p>
