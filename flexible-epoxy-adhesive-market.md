<p><p>The **Flexible Epoxy Adhesive Market** includes **One-component** and **Two-component Adhesives**. **One-component adhesives** are pre-mixed, ready-to-use formulations that cure with heat or moisture, offering convenience and ease of application. They are ideal for industries requiring quick, single-step bonding. **Two-component adhesives** consist of a resin and a hardener, mixed before use, providing superior strength, durability, and flexibility. They are preferred for demanding applications requiring precise control over curing time and performance. Both types cater to diverse industries like automotive, construction, and electronics, addressing specific bonding needs.</p></p>
