<p><p>The paint detackifier market is categorized into two main types: powder and liquid. Powder detackifiers offer concentrated formulations that can be easily mixed with solvents or water, providing versatility and ease of storage. They are ideal for large-volume applications and often have a longer shelf life. Both types effectively reduce paint tackiness, enhancing the efficiency of the painting process.</p></p>
