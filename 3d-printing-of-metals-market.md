<p><p>The 3D printing of metals market encompasses various techniques, including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses high-powered lasers to fuse metal powder layer-by-layer, enabling the production of complex geometries with high precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt the metal powder, offering advantages in speed and material density, particularly for titanium alloys. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, which expand application versatility in various industries.</p></p>
