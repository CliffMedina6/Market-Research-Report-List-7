<p><p>Polyurethane foam insulation market is categorized into three main types: flexible foam, spray foam, and rigid foam. Flexible foam, known for its softness and adaptability, is commonly used in furniture and automotive applications. Spray foam provides excellent air sealing and insulation in irregular spaces, making it ideal for residential and commercial buildings. Rigid foam, characterized by its high thermal resistance, is often used in sheathing and insulating walls, roofs, and foundations, offering structural support along with thermal efficiency.</p></p>
