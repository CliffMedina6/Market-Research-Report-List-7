<p><p>The ring gage market is categorized into three primary types: plain ring gages, taper ring gages, and thread ring gauges. Plain ring gages are used to check external dimensions of cylindrical parts without any taper, ensuring dimensional accuracy. Taper ring gages facilitate measurement of tapered surfaces, essential for applications requiring precise fit. Thread ring gauges verify the pitch and size of threaded parts, crucial for ensuring proper assembly. Each type serves specific quality control needs across various manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
