<p><strong>The PCB Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Soldering Oven</li><li>Selective Soldering Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering oven market encompasses three primary types: wave soldering ovens, reflow soldering ovens, and selective soldering ovens. Wave soldering ovens utilize a continuous wave of molten solder, ideal for mass production of through-hole components. Reflow soldering ovens employ controlled heating to melt solder paste, best suited for surface mount technology. Selective soldering ovens combine features of both, allowing precise solder application for specific components on a PCB, making them valuable for complex assemblies and mixed technologies.</p></p>
