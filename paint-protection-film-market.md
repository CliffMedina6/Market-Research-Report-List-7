<p><strong>The Paint Protection Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC Type</li><li>PU Type</li><li>TPU Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint protection film market is segmented into three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) films are cost-effective and easy to apply but may lack durability. PU (polyurethane) films offer better clarity and flexibility, providing good resistance against scratches and yellowing. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) films combine the best features of both, offering high durability, self-healing properties, and excellent performance against environmental elements. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for protection, cost, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
