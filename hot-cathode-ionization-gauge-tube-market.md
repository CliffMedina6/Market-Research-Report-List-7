<p><p>The Hot Cathode Ionization Gauge Tube market features two main types: Single Filament and Double Filament gauges. Single Filament gauges utilize a single heated filament to ionize gas molecules, producing a current proportional to pressure, often providing a cost-effective solution for low to medium vacuum measurements. In contrast, Double Filament gauges employ two filaments, allowing for enhanced sensitivity and accuracy by enabling differential measurements. This dual approach caters to more demanding applications, ensuring reliability in a broader range of vacuum conditions.</p></p>
