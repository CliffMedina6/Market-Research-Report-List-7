<p><p>The Instant Electric Water Heaters market is segmented into two primary types: Whole-House Instant Electric Water Heaters and Point-of-Use Instant Electric Water Heaters. Whole-House models deliver hot water to multiple outlets simultaneously, providing a consistent supply for entire households. In contrast, Point-of-Use units are installed near specific water outlets, delivering hot water instantly at the source, minimizing wait times and energy loss. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs regarding efficiency, convenience, and space utilization in residential or commercial settings.</p></p>
