<p><p>The cylinder regulators market comprises three main types: low pressure regulators, high pressure adjustable regulators, and middle pressure regulators. Low pressure regulators control gas flow at reduced pressure for applications like welding and heating. High pressure adjustable regulators manage higher pressures, allowing precise gas delivery for industrial uses. Middle pressure regulators cater to applications between the two extremes, offering versatility for various needs. Each type serves specific sectors, including medical, industrial, and commercial, ensuring safe and efficient gas management.</p></p>
