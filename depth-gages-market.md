Dial depth gages feature a mechanical dial indicator, providing visual readings with good accuracy, ideal for workshop environments. Vernier depth gages utilize a sliding scale for measurement, combining simplicity and precision; they are often used in educational settings and basic applications. Each type caters to specific user needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
