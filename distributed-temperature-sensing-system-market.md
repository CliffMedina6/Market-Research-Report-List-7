<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) systems are advanced sensing technologies that provide continuous temperature measurement along a fiber optic cable. This enables real-time monitoring of temperature variations over a long distance, making it highly valuable in various applications such as oil and gas, environmental monitoring, power generation, and infrastructure management. The system operates by analyzing the backscattered light in the fiber, converting temperature data into precise measurement points.</p><p>The Distributed Temperature Sensing System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) market is primarily segmented into two types: Single-Mode Fiber (SMF) and Multimode Fiber (MMF). Single-Mode Fiber utilizes a single light path for long-distance applications, offering high resolution and sensitivity, making it ideal for critical industrial and environmental monitoring. In contrast, Multimode Fiber allows multiple light paths, suited for shorter distances and cost-effective solutions. Each type caters to different operational needs, impacting sectors like energy, civil engineering, and environmental monitoring, thus shaping market dynamics.</p></p>
