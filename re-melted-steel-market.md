<p><ul><li>Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) Process</li><li>Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR) Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The re-melted steel market primarily focuses on two advanced processes: Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) and Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR). ESR involves melting steel using electrical resistance heating, resulting in high-quality ingots with improved purity and properties. VAR, on the other hand, utilizes an electric arc in a vacuum to eliminate impurities, producing superior alloyed steels. Both methods cater to industries requiring specialized materials, enhancing strength and performance, crucial for high-stress applications in aerospace, automotive, and energy sectors.</p></p>
