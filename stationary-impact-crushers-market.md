<p><strong>The Stationary Impact Crushers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Impact</li><li>Vertical Shaft Impact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary impact crushers market consists primarily of two types: Horizontal Shaft Impact (HSI) and Vertical Shaft Impact (VSI) crushers. HSI crushers utilize a horizontal rotor to accelerate material, making them effective for primary and secondary crushing of softer materials. Conversely, VSI crushers employ a vertical rotor, promoting high-speed material collision for shaping and fines production, ideal for producing crushed stone and sand. Both types serve various industries, including construction, mining, and recycling, with distinct applications and benefits.</p></p>
