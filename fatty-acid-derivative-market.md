<p><p>The fatty acid derivative market includes various types, notably supercritical fluid-derived fatty acids (SCFAD) and α-substituted fatty acid derivatives. SCFADs are obtained using supercritical fluid extraction, enhancing purity and functionality for applications in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. α-Substituted fatty acid derivatives involve modifications at the alpha position of the fatty acid chain, leading to unique properties that can improve performance in surfactants, lubricants, and intermediates for specialty chemicals. Both categories cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
