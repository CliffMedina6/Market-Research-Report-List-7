Positive connectors allow fluid flow only when a specific action is taken, minimizing accidental leaks. Negative connectors prevent backflow and air entry, enhancing safety. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing their adoption rates and impacting overall market dynamics in healthcare settings.</p></p>
