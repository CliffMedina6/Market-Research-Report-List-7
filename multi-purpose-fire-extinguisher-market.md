<p><p>The multi-purpose fire extinguisher market includes primarily two types: Multi-purpose Powder Fire Extinguishers and Multi-purpose Foam Fire Extinguishers. Multi-purpose Powder extinguishers utilize dry chemical agents, effective against Class A (ordinary combustibles), B (flammable liquids), and C (gases) fires, making them versatile for various settings. Multi-purpose Foam extinguishers, on the other hand, use foam to suppress fires, primarily Class A and B, and are particularly effective on flammable liquid fires. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing safety across diverse environments.</p></p>
