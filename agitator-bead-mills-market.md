<p><p>Agitator bead mills are essential in the milling process, particularly for dispersing and grinding solid materials. The market is divided into two main types: dry bead mills and wet bead mills. Dry bead mills operate without liquid, enabling the processing of materials with minimal moisture content, ideal for powders. In contrast, wet bead mills utilize liquids to disperse particles, enhancing the grinding efficiency and producing finer particles, making them suitable for coatings, inks, and pharmaceuticals. Each type caters to specific industry needs.</p></p>
