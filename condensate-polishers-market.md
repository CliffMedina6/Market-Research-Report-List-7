<p><strong>The Condensate Polishers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixed Bed Condensate Polishers</li><li>Cation Condensate Polishers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The condensate polishers market is primarily divided into two types: mixed bed condensate polishers and cation condensate polishers. Mixed bed condensate polishers utilize a combination of both cation and anion resins in a single vessel, effectively removing a wide range of impurities from condensate water for high-purity applications. In contrast, cation condensate polishers focus solely on removing cations, serving as a crucial pre-treatment step for processes that require highly purified water, especially in power generation and other industrial sectors.</p></p>
