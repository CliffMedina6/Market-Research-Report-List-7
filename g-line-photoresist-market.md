<p><ul><li>Photopolymerization</li><li>Light Decomposition Type</li><li>Photocrosslinked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The G-line photoresist market comprises three main types: Photopolymerization, Light Decomposition Type, and Photocrosslinked. Photopolymerization involves materials that change chemical structure upon exposure to light, enabling precise patterning in semiconductor manufacturing. Light Decomposition Type photoresists break down upon exposure, releasing substances that facilitate the development process. Photocrosslinked photoresists form three-dimensional networks when exposed to light, enhancing thermal stability and resolution. Each type caters to specific requirements in microfabrication, influencing applications in electronics, optics, and various advanced technologies.</p></p>
