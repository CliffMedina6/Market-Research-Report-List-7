<p><p>The pouring mixer market is categorized into fixed type and mobile type mixers. Fixed type mixers are stationary units, typically used in large-scale construction sites where steady, high-volume mixing is required. They often feature advanced automation and are integrated into a permanent setup. Their convenience makes them ideal for smaller projects or areas with limited space and equipment accessibility.</p></p>
