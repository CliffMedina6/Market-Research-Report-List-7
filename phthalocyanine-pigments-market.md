<p><p>The Phthalocyanine Pigments market is primarily divided into two types: Metal Phthalocyanine Pigments and Metal Free Phthalocyanine Pigments. Metal Phthalocyanine Pigments contain metal elements such as copper or aluminum, providing vibrant colors and excellent lightfastness, making them suitable for a variety of applications including coatings and plastics.
