<p><p>The food-grade soda ash market encompasses two main types: light soda ash and dense soda ash. Light soda ash, characterized by its fluffy texture and lower bulk density, is often used in applications requiring rapid dissolution and increased solubility. In contrast, dense soda ash is more compact and has a higher bulk density, making it suitable for applications demanding greater stability and easier handling. Both types are essential in industries like food processing, where purity and consistency are crucial.</p></p>
