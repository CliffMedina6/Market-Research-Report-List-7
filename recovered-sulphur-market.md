<p><p>The recovered sulfur market encompasses various processes for extracting sulfur, primarily focusing on the Claus process and tail gas treatment. The Claus process involves converting hydrogen sulfide (H2S) into elemental sulfur through combustion, making it vital for oil and gas industries. Tail gas treatment complements the Claus process by further refining the remaining sulfur compounds, ensuring minimal environmental impact. Together, these processes enable efficient sulfur recovery, contributing to sustainability and meeting regulatory requirements while maximizing economic value from natural resources.</p></p>
