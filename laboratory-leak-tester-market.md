<p><p>The Laboratory Leak Tester market is segmented into two primary types: the Hammering Method and Ultrasound testing. The Hammering Method involves creating sound waves by striking the test surface, detecting leaks based on the response of the material. Ultrasound testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to identify leaks by listening for sound anomalies in pressurized systems. Both methods are crucial for ensuring the integrity of laboratory equipment and environments by providing reliable, non-destructive testing solutions to detect potential leaks.</p></p>
