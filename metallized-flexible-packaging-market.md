<p><p>The metallized flexible packaging market encompasses two primary types: aluminum foil-based flexible packaging and metallized film flexible packaging. Aluminum foil-based packaging utilizes thin layers of aluminum to create barriers against moisture, light, and oxygen, enhancing product shelf life. Metallized film packaging, on the other hand, involves a polymer film coated with a thin layer of metal, often aluminum, providing similar protective qualities while being lighter and more versatile. Both types are widely used across food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
