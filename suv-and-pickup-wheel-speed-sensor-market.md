<p><p>The SUV and pickup wheel speed sensor market includes two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, providing precise measurements essential for anti-lock braking systems and traction control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors generate voltage through a magnetic field to monitor wheel rotation, offering reliability and durability. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety, performance, and fuel efficiency, catering to the growing demand for advanced automotive technologies in SUVs and pickups.</p></p>
