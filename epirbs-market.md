<p><p>EPIRBs, or Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacons, are classified into three main market types based on their communication protocols. COSPAS-SARSAT Type beacons utilize satellite systems for global distress signal transmission, ensuring rapid location identification. NMARSAT E Type beacons operate through maritime satellite networks, enhancing rescue coordination for vessels. VHF CH 70 Type EPIRBs communicate via the VHF radio frequency, providing local search and rescue teams with a reliable means to receive distress signals within coastal areas. Each type serves essential safety functions in marine environments.</p></p>
