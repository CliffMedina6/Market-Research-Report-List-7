<p><p>The glass frit powder market is categorized into two primary types: coarse powder and fine powder. Coarse powder typically ranges from 0.5mm to 2mm and is used in applications like ceramics and glass production, offering enhanced durability and texture. Fine powder, on the other hand, has particle sizes below 0.5mm, making it suitable for advanced applications such as coatings, composites, and artistic glasswork, where precise finishes and homogeneous mixtures are essential. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
