<p><strong>The Rebar Micropiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Drilling Micropiles</li><li>Grouted Micropiles</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rebar micropiles are categorized into several types based on their installation methods and structural applications. Self-drilling micropiles are installed by using a hollow bar that simultaneously drills and grouts into the ground, providing immediate load-bearing capacity. Grouted micropiles rely on pre-drilled holes filled with grout, enhancing stability and resisting settlement. Other market types include composite micropiles, which use a combination of materials for enhanced performance, and tension micropiles designed for uplift resistance in structures. Each type serves specific geotechnical needs.</p></p>
