<p><ul><li>Linear Spray Valves</li><li>Radial Spray Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial spray valves market features two primary types: linear and radial spray valves. Linear spray valves deliver a straight, controlled spray pattern, ideal for precise applications such as coating and cleaning. These are often used in processes requiring accuracy. Radial spray valves, on the other hand, produce a circular spray pattern, suitable for surface coverage in larger areas. They are commonly utilized in applications like agricultural spraying and cooling systems, offering efficient distribution across varied surfaces.</p></p>
