<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market comprises two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and integrated circuits (ICs) and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers manage the delivery of power over Ethernet cables to devices like IP cameras and wireless access points, ensuring efficient energy distribution. Conversely, PD controllers and ICs are integrated into powered devices to manage power intake, control, and utilization, enhancing device functionality and energy efficiency within network systems.</p></p>
