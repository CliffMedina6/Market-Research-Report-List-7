<p><strong>The PVC Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC paste market features two primary types: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves dissolving PVC resin in a low molecular weight plasticizer, creating a fine suspension that offers higher stability and improved mechanical properties. In contrast, the Emulsion Method utilizes water and surfactants to disperse PVC particles, resulting in a creamy consistency ideal for various applications. Both methods are critical in enhancing the versatility and performance of PVC paste products across industries.</p></p>
