<p><p>The wastewater and water treatment equipment market encompasses various technologies aimed at purifying water for safe consumption and environmental protection. Media filtration equipment uses physical barriers, such as sand or gravel, to remove particles and contaminants from water. In contrast, membrane filtration equipment utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate impurities based on size, effectively targeting smaller contaminants and pathogens. Both types of equipment are critical in ensuring efficient water treatment processes across industrial, municipal, and residential applications.</p></p>
