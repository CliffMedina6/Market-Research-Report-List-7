<p><p>The die cutting machines market is primarily categorized into three types: rotary die cutting machines, platen die cutting machines, and others. Rotary die cutting machines utilize a cylindrical die to produce precise cuts and shapes at high speeds, making them ideal for large-scale production. Platen die cutting machines use a flat surface to apply pressure, allowing for intricate designs and thicker materials. The "others" category encompasses alternative methods like laser die cutting and digital die cutting, catering to diverse applications and materials.</p></p>
