<p><p>The endoscope lens market comprises two main types: naked lenses and lens modules. Naked lenses are single optical components used in endoscopes to provide visibility within the body, often requiring additional assembly for functionality. In contrast, lens modules are pre-assembled units that combine multiple optical elements, offering enhanced imaging quality and ease of integration into endoscopic systems. These types cater to varying medical needs, with naked lenses focusing on simplicity and modular systems emphasizing high performance and versatility.</p></p>
