<p><p>The adjustable output voltage regulator market is primarily segmented into two main types: single output and multiple outputs. Single output regulators provide a single adjustable voltage level, ideal for applications requiring a specific voltage supply, such as consumer electronics. In contrast, multiple output regulators deliver several adjustable voltage levels from one unit, making them suitable for complex systems, such as industrial equipment or communication devices, where different components operate at varying voltage requirements. Both types play crucial roles in power management.</p></p>
