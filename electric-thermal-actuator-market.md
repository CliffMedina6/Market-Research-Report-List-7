<p><strong>The Electric Thermal Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric thermal actuator market is primarily segmented into two types: Normally Closed (NC) and Normally Open (NO). Normally Closed types remain shut when not energized, ensuring systems stay sealed until activated, making them ideal for safety applications. Conversely, Normally Open types are open when de-energized, allowing immediate flow when power is applied, suitable for applications requiring quick responsiveness. These types cater to various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing, offering distinct functionalities based on operational requirements.</p></p>
