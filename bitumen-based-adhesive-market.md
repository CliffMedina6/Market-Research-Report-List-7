<p><ul><li>Liquid State</li><li>Paste</li><li>Solid State</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bitumen-based adhesive market is segmented into three main types: liquid state, paste, and solid state. Liquid state adhesives are easily spreadable and ideal for large surface applications, providing strong adhesion when cured. Paste adhesives have a thicker consistency, suitable for vertical applications and offering high initial tack. Solid state adhesives, often in the form of sheets or blocks, are used for specialized applications requiring robust bonding strength in extreme conditions. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
