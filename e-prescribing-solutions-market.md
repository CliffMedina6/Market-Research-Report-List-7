<p><p>E-prescribing solutions are categorized into two main market types: web-based and on-premise. Web-based e-prescribing systems are accessible via the internet, allowing healthcare providers to send prescriptions from any location with internet connectivity. This model often features lower upfront costs and easier updates. Conversely, on-premise solutions are installed directly within a healthcare facility's infrastructure. They offer greater control over data and security but typically involve higher initial costs and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to enhance prescription accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
