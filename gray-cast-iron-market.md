<p><ul><li>Ferrite</li><li>Ferrite-Pearlite</li><li>Pearlite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gray cast iron market is categorized into three main types: ferrite, ferrite-pearlite, and pearlite. Ferrite gray cast iron, with a softer and ductile structure, is used where high shock resistance is required. Ferrite-pearlite combines both phases, offering a balance of strength and machinability, making it suitable for automotive components. Pearlite gray cast iron, being the hardest and strongest, is primarily applied in high-stress environments like heavy machinery and industrial applications, where durability and wear resistance are critical.</p></p>
