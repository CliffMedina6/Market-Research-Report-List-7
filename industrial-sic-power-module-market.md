<p><p>The Industrial SiC Power Module market is primarily divided into two types: Hybrid SiC Modules and Full SiC Modules. Hybrid SiC Modules combine silicon and silicon carbide components, offering a cost-effective solution with enhanced efficiency and thermal management. Conversely, Full SiC Modules utilize entirely silicon carbide materials, delivering superior performance, higher switching speeds, and better thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-power applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for energy efficiency in industrial settings.</p></p>
