<p><p>The agricultural tractors market is divided into two main types: two-wheel drive (2WD) and four-wheel drive (4WD) tractors. Two-wheel drive tractors are typically lighter and more economical, suited for flat terrain and smaller farms, offering easier maneuverability. In contrast, four-wheel drive tractors provide superior traction and stability, making them ideal for rough or uneven terrains, heavy tillage, and larger agricultural operations. Each type caters to specific farming needs, influencing their adoption based on farm size and environmental conditions.</p></p>
