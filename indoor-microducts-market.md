<p><p>The indoor microducts market is divided into two main types: thick wall microducts and thin-walled microducts. Thick wall microducts are designed for robust applications, offering enhanced durability and protection against physical stresses, making them suitable for environments with high installation demands. In contrast, thin-walled microducts prioritize flexibility and lightweight properties, allowing for easier installation in tight spaces. Both types serve crucial roles in efficiently housing and organizing fiber optic cables for telecommunications and data management systems within indoor settings.</p></p>
