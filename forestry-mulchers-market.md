<p><ul><li>Mounted Mulcher</li><li>Offset Mulcher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forestry mulchers market primarily includes mounted mulchers and offset mulchers. Mounted mulchers are attached directly to a tractor or skid steer, providing significant maneuverability and are ideal for smaller to medium-sized clearing projects. In contrast, offset mulchers extend beyond the vehicle's width, allowing operators to clear vegetation along roadways and fence lines without driving through the dense underbrush. Both types enhance land management efficiency, promoting effective vegetation control and soil preservation in diverse forestry applications.</p></p>
