<p><p>The Plate Frame Heat Exchanger market comprises two main types: Gasketed Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers and Welded Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers. Gasketed models feature removable gaskets, allowing for easy maintenance and adaptability to various applications, making them ideal for low-pressure conditions. In contrast, welded models, which have no gaskets, offer higher durability and efficiency for more demanding environments, particularly at elevated pressures and temperatures. Together, they cater to diverse industrial heating and cooling needs across sectors.</p></p>
