<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market comprises three main types: Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip. Lateral Chips feature a horizontal arrangement, providing efficient heat dissipation and compact designs. Vertical Chips are aligned upright, enhancing light extraction and thermal performance, making them suitable for high-brightness applications. Flip Chips utilize an inverted structure, connecting directly to the substrate, which improves electrical performance and reduces thermal resistance. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to specific lighting applications and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
