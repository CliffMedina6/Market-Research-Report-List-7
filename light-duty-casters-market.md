Swivel casters feature a rotating wheel design, allowing for enhanced maneuverability, ideal for navigating tight spaces. In contrast, rigid casters have fixed wheels that provide stable, straight-line movement, making them suitable for stationary applications where direction changes are infrequent. Both types are essential in various industries for applications such as furniture, carts, and equipment, balancing flexibility and stability based on specific needs.</p></p>
