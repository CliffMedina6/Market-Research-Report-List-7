<p><p>The polyurethane thickener market is primarily divided into powdered and liquid types. Powdered thickeners are dry formulations that are mixed with solvents or dispersants to enhance viscosity, offering versatility in various applications. Liquid thickeners, on the other hand, are ready-to-use solutions that provide immediate viscosity improvements, often utilized in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Each type serves distinct requirements, with powdered options favored for storage and transport ease, while liquid variants are preferred for consistent performance in formulations.</p></p>
