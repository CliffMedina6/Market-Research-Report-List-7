<p><p>The anti-pollution reverse osmosis membrane market is characterized by three primary types: cellulose acetate film, aromatic polyamide film, and composite reverse osmosis membrane. Cellulose acetate film is known for its low fouling tendency and biodegradability, making it suitable for various water purification applications. Aromatic polyamide film offers higher permeability and chemical resistance, ideal for demanding industrial uses. Composite reverse osmosis membranes combine the benefits of both materials, providing enhanced performance and durability, effectively addressing water contamination challenges in diverse sectors.</p></p>
