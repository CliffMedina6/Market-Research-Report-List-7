<p><p>The flexible thermal insulation market comprises several key types, each based on different materials. NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based insulation offers excellent thermal resistance and is widely used in refrigeration. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based insulation provides outstanding weather resistance and is suitable for outdoor applications. Chloroprene based insulation is known for its durability and is often used in industrial settings. Other materials include polyurethane and polyethylene, which cater to specific needs in various industries, enhancing energy efficiency and temperature control.</p></p>
