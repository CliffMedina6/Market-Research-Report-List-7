<p><p>Flow wrap machines are categorized into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal flow wrap machines operate by wrapping products in a horizontal plane, making them ideal for packaging flat or irregularly shaped items like snacks or baked goods. Vertical flow wrap machines, on the other hand, wrap products in a vertical orientation, suitable for packaging items like pouches or standing bags. Both types enhance packaging efficiency and are crucial in various industries, from food to consumer goods.</p></p>
