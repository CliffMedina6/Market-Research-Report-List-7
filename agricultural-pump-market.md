<p><p>The agricultural pump market encompasses various types, each serving specific irrigation needs. Submersible pumps operate underwater, efficiently lifting water from wells or boreholes. Self-priming pumps facilitate water movement by creating a vacuum, ideal for surface water irrigation. Vortex pumps handle liquids with solids, reducing clogging, making them suitable for dirty water applications. Other pumps in the market may include centrifugal and diaphragm pumps, each offering unique benefits for diverse agricultural practices, enhancing productivity and water management.</p></p>
