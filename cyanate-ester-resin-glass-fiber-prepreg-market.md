<p><p>Cyanate ester resin glass fiber prepreg is primarily categorized into two market types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs undergo a curing process that forms a rigid structure, providing excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications like aerospace and automotive. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs offer flexibility and reprocessability, allowing for easier shaping and recycling. This versatility makes them appealing for applications requiring rapid production and repairs, such as in the consumer goods and electronics sectors.</p></p>
