<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive audio amplifier market features three primary types: Class A, Class B, and Class A/B amplifiers. Class A amplifiers are known for their high sound quality and low distortion, operating continuously; however, they are less energy-efficient. Class B amplifiers, on the other hand, improve efficiency by amplifying only half the audio signal, resulting in higher distortion levels. Class A/B amplifiers combine the benefits of both, offering a balance of sound quality and efficiency, making them popular in automotive applications.</p></p>
