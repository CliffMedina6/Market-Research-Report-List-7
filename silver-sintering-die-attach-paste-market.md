<p><p>The Silver Sintering Die Attach Paste market comprises two main types: pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. Pressure sintering involves applying mechanical pressure during the sintering process to enhance the densification of the silver paste, leading to improved thermal and electrical conductivity. In contrast, pressure-less sintering relies solely on temperature to achieve sintering, making it simpler and more cost-effective. Both methods are essential for optimizing device performance in applications such as power electronics and high-frequency modules.</p></p>
