<p><ul><li>Active Temperature Controlled System</li><li>Passive Temperature Controlled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature Controlled Packaging for Pharmaceuticals encompasses two main market types: Active and Passive Temperature Controlled Systems. Active systems utilize external energy sources, such as refrigeration or electronic controls, to maintain a stable temperature throughout transportation and storage. In contrast, Passive systems rely on insulating materials and phase change materials to regulate temperature, without needing external energy. Both systems aim to ensure the integrity and efficacy of temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals during distribution, catering to the varying needs of the pharmaceutical supply chain.</p></p>
