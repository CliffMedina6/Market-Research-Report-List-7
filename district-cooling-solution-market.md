<p><p>District cooling solutions encompass various types based on their energy sources. Electricity-powered systems utilize chillers driven by electric compressors to provide cooling. Steam-based systems use steam from centralized plants to drive absorption chillers, offering an efficient alternative. Sea water systems leverage coastal resources, using seawater to extract heat, which is particularly effective in coastal areas. Additionally, other methods may include using renewable energy sources or waste heat recovery systems, enhancing efficiency and sustainability in cooling delivery.</p></p>
