<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel gate valves market is categorized primarily into flanged and threaded connection types. Flanged connection valves feature wide, flat ends that secure tightly with bolts to pipes, ensuring leak-proof seals and accommodating higher pressure applications. Conversely, threaded connection valves have internal or external threads that allow for direct screwing onto pipes, providing ease of installation and compact designs suitable for low-pressure systems. Both types serve vital roles across various industries for regulating fluid flow effectively.</p></p>
