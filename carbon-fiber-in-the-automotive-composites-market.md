<p><p>In the automotive composites market, carbon fiber is utilized in various forms. Long fiber thermoplastics offer high strength and flexibility, making them ideal for structural components. Sheet molding compounds combine carbon fiber with resin for durable, molded parts. Short fiber thermoplastics provide lightweight solutions where high impact resistance is needed. Prepregs consist of carbon fiber pre-impregnated with resin, ensuring superior performance and consistency, often used in high-end applications for their exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and stiffness.</p></p>
