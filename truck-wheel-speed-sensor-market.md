<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Truck Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck wheel speed sensor market is primarily divided into Hall type and magnetic electric type sensors. </p><p>Hall type sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, producing a consistent output signal, making them highly reliable. They are commonly used in advanced vehicle systems. </p><p>Magnetic electric type sensors, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic induction to determine speed. They are simpler and more cost-effective but may offer less precision. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
