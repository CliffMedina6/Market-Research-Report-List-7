<p><p>The automotive taillights market includes various types such as incandescent lamps, LEDs, HID, and neon tubes. Incandescent lamps are traditional, cost-effective, and use heated filaments to produce light. LEDs are energy-efficient, durable, and offer faster response times, making them increasingly popular. HID lights provide brighter illumination with lower power consumption but are more expensive. Neon tubes, known for their unique glow and aesthetic appeal, are less common due to higher costs and fragility. Each type caters to different performance, design, and cost preferences in vehicle lighting.</p></p>
