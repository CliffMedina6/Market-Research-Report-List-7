<p><p>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive Film (ACF) is utilized for bonding and conducting electricity between electronic components. The market features two primary types: Double Layer ACF, consisting of an insulating layer with conductive particles, ensuring vertical conductivity while preventing lateral flow; and Three Layers ACF, which adds a third insulation layer for enhanced performance and reliability. Both types cater to the increasing demand for miniaturization in electronics, offering solutions for efficient electrical connections in devices such as displays and semiconductor packages.</p></p>
