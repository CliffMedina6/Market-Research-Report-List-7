<p><strong>The Carbon Steel Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Weld Neck Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Socket Weld Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel flanges market includes various types essential for piping systems. Blind flanges serve to seal off the ends of pipes, preventing flow. Weld neck flanges provide strong connections through welding, ideal for high-pressure applications. Slip-on flanges allow simple installation by sliding over the pipe before welding. Socket weld flanges offer a secure fit for smaller diameter piping. Other types may include lap joint, threaded, and forged flanges, catering to diverse industrial needs and specifications.</p></p>
