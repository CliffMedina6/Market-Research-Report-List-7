<p><p>The isothermal humidifier market is divided mainly into two types: gas-fired and electric. Gas-fired humidifiers utilize natural gas or propane to generate steam, providing efficient humidity control, especially in large spaces. They are ideal for industries requiring high humidity levels. In contrast, electric humidifiers use electrical resistance to heat water, producing steam for humidification. They are typically more suitable for smaller applications and residential use, offering easier installation and maintenance. Both types cater to specific needs based on efficiency and application.</p></p>
