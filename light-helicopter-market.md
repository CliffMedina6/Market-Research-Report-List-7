<p><p>The light helicopter market includes piston-engined and turboshaft-engined models. Piston-engined helicopters are cost-effective, fuel-efficient, and ideal for training, personal use, or short-range operations. They are simpler in design but have lower power and performance capabilities. Turboshaft-engined helicopters, on the other hand, offer higher power, reliability, and versatility, making them suitable for commercial, emergency, and military applications. While more expensive to operate and maintain, they excel in demanding environments and longer missions. Both types cater to distinct needs within the light helicopter segment.</p></p>
