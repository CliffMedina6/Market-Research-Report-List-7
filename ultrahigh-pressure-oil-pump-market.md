<p><p>The ultrahigh pressure oil pump market comprises three main types: straight type, distribution type, and monomer type. </p><p>The straight type delivers a continuous flow of oil at high pressure, ideal for direct applications. The distribution type, on the other hand, is designed for multi-point delivery, ensuring efficient oil distribution across various systems. Lastly, the monomer type focuses on single units for specialized tasks or limited applications, offering flexibility in operations. Each type serves distinct needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
