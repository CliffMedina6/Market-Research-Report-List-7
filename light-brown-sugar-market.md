<p><p>The light brown sugar market comprises two main types: natural brown sugar and commercial brown sugar. Natural brown sugar is minimally processed, retaining molasses and nutrients, giving it a richer flavor and color. It’s often favored for organic and natural food products. In contrast, commercial brown sugar undergoes refining, where molasses is added back to white sugar, resulting in a uniform texture and sweetness. This type is widely used in baking and food manufacturing due to its cost-effectiveness and consistency.</p></p>
