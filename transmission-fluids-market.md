<p><p>The transmission fluids market encompasses three main types: mineral, synthetic, and semi-synthetic fluids. </p><p>Mineral fluids are derived from refining crude oil, offering basic lubricating properties at a lower cost. Synthetic fluids, formulated from chemical compounds, provide superior performance, enhanced thermal stability, and longevity, but at a higher price point. Semi-synthetic fluids blend mineral and synthetic bases, aiming to deliver a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements, reflecting diverse consumer preferences for performance, durability, and budget.</p></p>
