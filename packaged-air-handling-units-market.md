<p><p>Packaged Air Handling Units (AHUs) are self-contained systems designed to regulate and circulate air as part of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. This design simplifies installation and maintenance, making them suitable for various applications, including commercial buildings, hospitals, and industrial facilities.</p><p>The Packaged Air Handling Units Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
