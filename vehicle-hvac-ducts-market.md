<p><p>The Vehicle HVAC Ducts market includes Gas Ducts and Liquid Ducts, both essential for climate control systems. Liquid Ducts circulate coolant or refrigerant, crucial for heat exchange processes in HVAC systems. These ducts are typically made from materials resistant to corrosion and high pressure. Both types ensure efficient thermal management, enhancing passenger comfort and vehicle performance.</p></p>
