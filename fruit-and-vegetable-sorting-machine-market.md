<p><p>The fruit and vegetable sorting machine market can be categorized into three main types: Optical Sorting Machines, Gravity/Weight Sorting Machines, and Other Sorting Machines. Optical sorting machines utilize camera and imaging technology to identify and separate produce based on color, size, and defects, ensuring high precision. Gravity or weight sorting machines classify products based on their weight, facilitating the handling of various shapes and sizes. Other sorting machines may include mechanical or manual systems, catering to niche requirements and specific types of produce.</p></p>
