<p><p>The Rare Earth Luminescent Materials Market encompasses various types of luminescence. Photoluminescence involves the emission of light from a material after absorbing photons, commonly used in display technologies. Electroluminescence refers to light emitted via an electric field, widely applied in LEDs. Radiation luminescence occurs when materials emit light following exposure to radiation. Chemiluminescence involves chemical reactions yielding light, seen in glow sticks. Bioluminescence relates to light produced by living organisms, with potential applications in bioimaging. Other types encompass emerging luminescent technologies.</p></p>
