<p><p>The floor grinding tools market is primarily segmented into dry polishing and wet polishing types. Dry polishing utilizes diamond grinding pads without water, offering convenience and faster results, making it suitable for quick finishing tasks. In contrast, wet polishing involves the use of water to cool the surface and minimize dust, resulting in a smoother finish and reduced air pollution. Both methods cater to different preferences and project requirements, with distinct advantages in efficiency, finish quality, and environmental impact.</p></p>
