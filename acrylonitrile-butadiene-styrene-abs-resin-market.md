<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) resin markets primarily consist of two types: emulsion graft copolymerization and bulk copolymerization. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves creating ABS in an aqueous environment, resulting in finer particle sizes and improved toughness, making it suitable for applications like automotive parts. In contrast, bulk copolymerization occurs in a non-aqueous environment, producing a denser and more durable material, ideal for consumer goods and electronic housings. Both methods cater to diverse industry needs and performance specifications.</p></p>
